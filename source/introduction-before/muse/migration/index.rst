How to Redeem your MUSE/NOTE
===========================

This migration tutorial is relevant only to those customers and investors that
have participated in the MUSE pre-sale. We here give assistance for claiming
your funds in MUSE.

.. note:: The *NOTE blockchain* has been renamed to **MUSE blockchain** and the
          corresponding blockchain token is now called **MUSE**.

	
----------
